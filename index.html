Our Story

Two Decades of
Kashmiri Hospitality

Based in the heart of Srinagar, we are a locally owned and operated travel agency with over 25 years of experience in Kashmir tourism. Our dedicated team of travel experts curates personalised journeys for every kind of traveller — whether you seek a peaceful retreat on a houseboat, a romantic honeymoon amidst snow-capped peaks, or an adventure-packed trek through alpine meadows.

We specialise in customised Kashmir tour packages, houseboat stays on Dal & Nigeen Lake, hotel bookings across J&K, guided sightseeing, and adventure services including trekking, skiing, river rafting, and Jeep safaris in Leh-Ladakh.
