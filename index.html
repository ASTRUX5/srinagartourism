Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit Kashmir?

April to October is the main tourist season. Spring brings blooming gardens. October is golden with chinar leaves. December to February brings snowfall and skiing season in Gulmarg.

How much does a Kashmir tour package cost?

Packages start from ₹6,500 per person for a 3-night Srinagar tour. A 5-night Kashmir Special covering all major destinations costs around ₹9,500 per person. Honeymoon packages start at ₹30,500 per couple.

Is Kashmir safe for tourists?

Yes. Srinagar has well-established tourist infrastructure and thousands of families, couples, and solo travellers visit every year. We have been operating since 1999 with over 736 verified Google reviews.

Do you arrange houseboat stays?

Yes. We work with the finest houseboats on Dal and Nigeen Lake including Jigar Palace and Royal Dandoo Palace. Houseboat stays are included in most Srinagar and Kashmir packages.

Do you offer Ladakh tour packages?

Yes. Leh Ladakh packages start at ₹18,000 per person covering Pangong Lake, Nubra Valley, Khardungla Pass, and monasteries. We also offer a combined Kashmir with Ladakh tour.
