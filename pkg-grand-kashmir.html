About This Package

Seven nights in Kashmir leaves nothing out. Every major destination, every medium-distance valley, and two of the quiet places that most shorter tours skip: Yousmarg and Doodhpathri. This is the right choice for people who have the time and want to see the valley properly, or who are returning to Kashmir and ready to go further than the standard circuit. Two nights in Srinagar, a day each for Sonamarg and Gulmarg, two nights in Pahalgam, and on the final days Yousmarg and Doodhpathri back to Srinagar.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1
Arrival in Srinagar — Mughal Gardens and Shikara Ride

Arrive at Srinagar Airport and transfer to your hotel or houseboat. Visit the Mughal Gardens: Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, Chashme Shahi, and Pari Mahal. Evening 1-hour Shikara ride on Dal Lake (included). Overnight in Srinagar.

Airport ArrivalShalimar Bagh and Nishat BaghChashme Shahi and Pari Mahal1-Hour Shikara Ride on Dal Lake
Day 2
Srinagar Sightseeing — Hazratbal, Shankaracharya, Old City

Full day in Srinagar. Hazratbal Shrine on the western shore of Dal Lake. Shankaracharya Temple (200 BC) on the hilltop with panoramic views. Old city: Jamia Masjid, Shah Hamdan Shrine, Nowhatta lanes with Pashmina weavers and walnut carvers. Evening on Boulevard Road. Overnight in Srinagar.

Hazratbal ShrineShankaracharya Temple (200 BC)Jamia MasjidNowhatta Old City
Day 3
Day Trip to Sonamarg — Thajiwas Glacier

Drive to Sonamarg (80 km, 2.5 hrs). Thajiwas Glacier by pony or foot (own cost). Sindh River scenic spots. Optional rafting. Return to Srinagar overnight.

Sonamarg Meadow (2,740m)Thajiwas GlacierSindh River Valley
Day 4
Day Trip to Gulmarg — Gondola and Apharwat Peak

Drive to Gulmarg (50 km, 2 hrs). Gondola to Kongdoori Phase 1 (3,050m) and Apharwat Peak Phase 2 (4,200m) at own cost. World's highest golf course. Return to Srinagar overnight.

Gulmarg Meadow (2,650m)Gondola Phase 1 and Phase 2Apharwat Peak (4,200m)
Day 5
Drive to Pahalgam — Betaab Valley

Drive to Pahalgam (90 km, 2.5 hrs) via saffron fields and Awantipur Ruins. Afternoon in Betaab Valley (15 km). Union taxi required for local sightseeing (own cost). Overnight in Pahalgam.

Saffron Fields of PamporeAwantipur RuinsPahalgam (2,130m)Betaab Valley
Day 6
Pahalgam Full Day — Baisaran, Aru Valley, Chandanwari

Full day by union taxi / pony (own cost). Aru Valley (12 km), Betaab Valley (15 km), Chandanwari (15 km, Amarnath Yatra base). Optional: Baisaran (Mini Switzerland, pony ride). Overnight in Pahalgam.

Aru Valley (12 km)Betaab Valley (15 km)Chandanwari (15 km)Baisaran Mini Switzerland (pony)
Day 7
Pahalgam → Yousmarg → Doodhpathri → Srinagar

Last day on the road. Drive from Pahalgam toward Srinagar via two valleys most people miss. Yousmarg (47 km from Srinagar, 2,400m): a quiet meadow in pine forest with the Doodh Ganga River and Nilang Lake. Far fewer tourists than Gulmarg. Doodhpathri (42 km from Srinagar, 2,730m): Valley of Milk, named after the milky-white Shaliganga River, wide flat meadow with wildflowers in May-June. Walk through Palmaidan. Return to Srinagar for the last overnight.

Yousmarg Meadow (2,400m)Nilang LakeDoodhpathri (2,730m)Shaliganga RiverPalmaidan PlateauReturn to Srinagar
Day 8
Departure from Srinagar

After breakfast, check out and transfer to Srinagar Airport for return journey. Tour concludes with cherished memories of Kashmir.

Hotel Check-outSrinagar Airport TransferEnd of Tour
