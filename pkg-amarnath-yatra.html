About This Package

The Amarnath Yatra is one of India's most significant Hindu pilgrimages. The Amarnath Cave at 3,888 metres contains a naturally formed ice Shivling that waxes and wanes with the lunar cycle, reaching its peak at Shravan Purnima. The yatra season runs July to August. Srinagar Tourism has been organising Amarnath Yatra tours since 1999 and handles all registration, transport, accommodation, and logistics.

Important: Mandatory SASB (Shri Amarnath Shrine Board) registration permit required. Medical fitness certificate required. Persons above 70 years and children below 13 are not permitted by shrine board rules. We assist with the registration process.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1
Arrival in Srinagar — Mughal Gardens and Shikara Ride

Arrive at Srinagar Airport and transfer to your hotel or houseboat. Visit the Mughal Gardens: Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, Chashme Shahi, and Pari Mahal. Evening 1-hour Shikara ride on Dal Lake (included). Overnight in Srinagar.

Airport ArrivalShalimar Bagh and Nishat BaghChashme Shahi and Pari Mahal1-Hour Shikara Ride on Dal Lake
Day 2
Srinagar → Sonamarg → Neelgrath Base Camp

Early morning departure from Srinagar. Drive to Sonamarg (80 km, 2.5 hrs) via the Sindh River valley. Continue to Neelgrath, the base camp for the Baltal route to Amarnath. Registration and permits verified at checkpoints. Overnight at Neelgrath in tents or registered accommodation.

Drive to Sonamarg (80 km)Continue to Neelgrath Base CampPermit VerificationCamp Overnight at Neelgrath
Day 3
Neelgrath → Amarnath Cave → Return

The Baltal route trek covers approximately 14 km to the Amarnath Cave at 3,888 metres. Early morning start. The route follows the Amarnath Nallah through high-altitude terrain. Panchtarni, where five streams meet, is the main waypoint at 3,657 metres. The cave is set in a rock face with the naturally formed Shivling rising from the cave floor. Return trek to Neelgrath same day. Drive back toward Srinagar. Overnight in Srinagar.

Trek from Neelgrath (14 km round trip)Panchtarni (5 Streams, 3,657m)Amarnath Cave (3,888m)Naturally Formed Ice ShivlingReturn Trek and Drive to Srinagar
Day 4
Departure from Srinagar

After breakfast, check out and transfer to Srinagar Airport for return journey. Tour concludes with cherished memories of Kashmir.
