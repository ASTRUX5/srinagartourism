About This Package

The Kashmir Special is the most popular 4-night package and the reason is straightforward: it covers everything in one trip. Srinagar for the lake and the gardens, Sonamarg for the glacier, Gulmarg for the Gondola ride, and Pahalgam for the valleys. Five days, four nights, all the major destinations. If you are visiting Kashmir for the first time and want a complete picture of the valley, this is the package to book.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1
Arrival in Srinagar — Mughal Gardens and Dal Lake Shikara

Arrive at Srinagar Airport and transfer to your hotel or houseboat on Dal Lake. After checking in and freshening up, head out for local sightseeing. The Mughal Gardens are the first stop: Shalimar Bagh, the grandest of the three, built by Emperor Jehangir in 1619; Nishat Bagh, twelve terraces rising toward the Zabarwan mountains; and Chashme Shahi, the smallest, fed by a natural spring with terraced lawns. Also visit Pari Mahal, the ruined seven-terraced garden on the Zabarwan hillside with a panoramic view of the entire Dal Lake basin. In the evening, enjoy a 1-hour Shikara ride on Dal Lake, included in your package. Overnight in Srinagar.

Airport Arrival and Hotel Check-inShalimar BaghNishat BaghChashme ShahiPari Mahal1-Hour Shikara Ride on Dal Lake
Day 2
Day Trip to Sonamarg — Thajiwas Glacier

After breakfast, drive to Sonamarg (Meadow of Gold), 80 km from Srinagar along the Sindh River valley, about 2.5 hours. Sonamarg at 2,740 metres is the last Kashmir Valley stop before Zoji La and Ladakh. The main excursion is the Thajiwas Glacier, 4 km from the main stand: ponies are available near the stand, or you can walk. The glacier valley has snow even in July and August. Scenic spots along the Sindh River are good for photography and picnics. Optional: white-water rafting (seasonal, own cost). Return to Srinagar by evening for overnight stay.

Sonamarg Meadow (2,740m)Sindh River Valley DriveThajiwas Glacier (by pony or on foot)Optional River Rafting
Day 3
Day Trip to Gulmarg — Gondola and Meadow

After breakfast, drive to Gulmarg (Meadow of Flowers), 50 km from Srinagar, about 2 hours. Gulmarg at 2,650 metres is one of Asia's premier ski destinations in winter and a wide green meadow in summer. The main activity is the Gulmarg Gondola (Asia's highest cable car), which climbs to Kongdoori at Phase 1 (3,050m) and Apharwat Peak at Phase 2 (4,200m). Gondola tickets are at own cost. At the top, enjoy snow activities, skiing in winter, or views of the Himalayan ranges in every direction. The world's highest natural golf course is at Gulmarg if you want a round. Return to Srinagar by evening. Overnight in Srinagar.

Gulmarg Meadow (2,650m)Gondola Phase 1: Kongdoori (3,050m)Gondola Phase 2: Apharwat Peak (4,200m)World's Highest Golf Course
Day 4
Drive to Pahalgam — Arrival and Betaab Valley

After breakfast, drive to Pahalgam (Valley of Shepherds), 90 km from Srinagar, about 2.5 hours. On the way, pass the saffron fields of Pampore and the Awantipur Ruins, a 1,100-year-old Vishnu temple complex. Pahalgam at 2,130 metres has the Lidder River running through it. After hotel check-in, visit Betaab Valley, 15 km from town on the Chandanwari road, named after the 1983 Bollywood film shot here. Crystal-clear streams, dense pine forests, and snow peaks framing the end of the valley. Outside vehicles are not permitted in Pahalgam for sightseeing — a union taxi is required (own cost). Overnight in Pahalgam.

Saffron Fields of Pampore (en route)Awantipur Ruins 1,100-year-old TemplePahalgam (2,130m)Betaab ValleyUnion Taxi for Local Sightseeing
Day 5
Departure from Srinagar

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and transfer to Srinagar Airport for your return journey. The tour ends here with cherished memories of Kashmir.
